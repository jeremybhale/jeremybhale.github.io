Immersion Escape Rooms in Charlottesville, VA offers a unique experience that inserts friends, family, and co-workers into highly detailed, multimedia environments where they must work together to find hidden meanings, solve puzzles, and unlock a meticulously designed story that extends across multiple sets and even beyond the 4th wall.

HOUSE ARREST

Your theory turned out to be true: a mysterious virus has quietly taken control of the world, and now it’s coming for you. Trapped inside your own apartment, hitmen are coming to shut you down for good. The clock is counting down, but an unexpected message from a stranger just might help you escape an untimely death.

Action

FOR THE LOVE OF SCIENCE

You’re safe for now, but you’ll need to do something for your new friend before he will trust you. In the lab where the virus was created, there are machines which will weaken its power. It’s up to you to sneak into the high security facility, power up the equipment, and escape before anyone knows you’re there.

Action

THE LION'S DEN

You’ve found yourself on the guest list of a party celebrating the privileged elite, but there’s no time to enjoy the hors d'oeuvres. It’s all part of an effort to get you into the private study of one of the wealthiest and dangerous men on the planet. It’s time to end his reign, but can you find the key before security forces find you?
