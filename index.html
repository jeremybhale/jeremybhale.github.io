Pet Projects

These are just a few of the things I've built over the last few years.

Space Invaders

I got to thinking about how little space was available on old arcade systems, and I wanted to see how small I could make something similar. It's not complete, and not quite as small as the original. Arrow keys move, space bar fires.

The Leshy

I love puzzles, scavenger hunts, and mysteries. One summer, I took a photography contest and turned it into an opportunity to start a little game for my friends. Finding all the hidden items would reveal a clue.

Pick Three

I participated in prototyping a gamified way to make studying more fun. This is a stripped down version of the game. Answering questions correctly earns points, and there's a chance to double your score.

Fill in the Trip

I was tired of seeing all these commercials for travel booking sites. They all said they were different, but I couldn't see it. How would it work if it was different? Maybe if you start out not knowing where you wanted to go.

Clara Walks

I've always like the idea of making cartoons. Usually I don't have the patience for it, but I liked the pig from the gamification project so I went for it. Try the left, right, and up arrow keys.

Locus Imperium

This is another example of a page I made for a puzzle, though this one came about several years ago. I was experimenting with navigation styles, and to this day I still load this up to spin the orbit with the mouse.

SVG Map

I worked on a project with a vendor who had a hard time keeping up with our requirements. I said to myself, "I could do this in an hour". It took a bit longer than an hour, but this basic SVG map worked a lot better than what was delivered.
